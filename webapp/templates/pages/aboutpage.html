THE SANCTUARY

CatZ Sanctuary was formed in 2023 when a group of animal lovers wanted a shelter for stray cats that were homeless. We provide shelter for stray, abandoned and unwanted cats in a safe, loving, cage free environment. There is a no kill policy at CatZ., therefore, any animals that are not adopted will be able to live the rest of their natural lives at the shelter.
